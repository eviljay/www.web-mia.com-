Who we are and what we make

We are small company making audiovisual programmes both in predeclared television format SD and a new High Definition standard which is suitable for analogue 35 mm film transcription as well. We produce architecture, art and technology documents, promotional and industry video movies, including video advertising, music video-clips, reports, dokumentatary and publicistic shots.

We cooperate with wide archive Video for Internet in area of technology and industry. We supply ready television programmes or pressed DVD for commercial applications. The customer's idea and conception is our basic clue.
